Let’s Build New Markets Together

This process is intended for serious business profiles with real capacity for distribution and market development.

Key Requirements (Self-Assessment)

Before continuing with the process, please confirm that you meet the following criteria:

  • ✓

    I am a business owner or represent a legally established company.

  • ✓

    I have an approximate minimum initial investment capacity of USD $180,000.00.

  • ✓

    I have experience or knowledge in nutraceutical products, supplements, or mass consumer goods.

  • ✓

    I have my own sales channels or an active distribution network.

  • ✓

    I can cover any analysis expenses in the target area (market study, surveys, etc.).

  • ✓

    I can start operations within a maximum of 60 days after signing the agreement.

If you do not meet most of these points, this distribution model may not be suitable for your profile.

Entry Conditions

  • Minimum initial purchase: 2 pallets.
  • Content per pallet: 2,256 bottles of 120 ml.
  • Total portfolio: 5 products.
  • Minimum repurchase commitment for one year (inventory).

Mandatory composition of the initial order:

  • 1 pallet of the best-selling product.
  • 1 pallet with a proportional assortment of the other four products.

Representation Model

  • Country-based representation (exclusive or non-exclusive, subject to evaluation).
  • Market development through own channels and/or third-party distribution.
  • Focus on rotation, brand positioning, and sustained growth.

Expected Commercial Launch

The interested party must present a basic launch strategy, including:

  • Product introduction strategy.
  • Priority sales channels.
  • Initial commercialization plan.

Next Step After Screening

Profiles that meet the criteria will advance to:

  1. Commercial and financial evaluation.
  2. Market development proposal presentation.
  3. Definition of commercial terms.
  4. Distribution agreement signing.

BIOMAUSSAN INTERNATIONAL is not looking for occasional buyers, but for strategic partners with real capacity for investment, execution, and growth.

If you believe you meet this profile and wish to move forward in the process, you may request a commercial evaluation meeting.
